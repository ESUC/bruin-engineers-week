Our Story

Engineers Week (E-Week) is an annual week-long series of events hosted by ESUC during Week 2 of Spring Quarter in celebration of all things engineering.

Our Plan

Numerous events involving volunteering, networking, problem-solving, dancing, expressing ideas, and having fun will be available for students to attend.

Our Vision

Events held during E-Week are designed to bring students together through innovation, outreach, and fun as well as promote individual development.

  • Kids Day

  • Networking Night

  • Rocket Egg Drop

  • Engineers Ball

  • Field Day

  • Art Display

Kids Day

Kids Day is an event for the UCLA Engineering Students to give back to the community. During Kids Day, hundreds of elementary school students are invited to UCLA for a day of hands-on learning outside of a classroom setting.

  • Date: April 11, 2023
  • Time: 8 AM to 1 PM
  • Location: Ackerman Grand Ballroom

Engineer's Week is a decades-old tradition at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) hosted by the Engineering Society of UCLA. During this week-long festivities, the UCLA engineering community gathers to celebrate the achievements of the engineering clubs and works together to give back to the greater community. One of the primary events is Kids Day, during which hundreds of elementary school students are invited to UCLA for a day of hands-on learning outside of a classroom setting. This is a rewarding experience for older students to volunteer their time to the community and help foster a love for learning and engineering in younger students.

Networking Night

An evening during E-Week, where representatives from different companies come to talk to UCLA students about the future, industry, and advice.

  • Date: April 11, 2023
  • Time: Evening
  • Location: Zoom

This smaller networking event is embedded in E-week and is designed to be a casual, informative evening for students to start or continue forming professional relationships with company representatives/alumni, as well as get advice. The goal of Networking Night is to help undergraduate students make connections with people in the industry. Undergraduate students are able to get insight into what it is like to work in the industry and how they can get internships.

Rocket Egg Drop

The goals of this engineering challenge are to allow students to demonstrate their newfound knowledge from classes and apply what they have learned in a fun, team-oriented environment.

  • Date: April 13, 2023
  • Time: 4 PM to 6 PM
  • Location: Boelter Penthouse (Floor 9)

In the spirit of E-Week, students will get the chance to show off their engineering skills through a classic egg drop with a twist. The egg will be launched off the balcony with a bottle rocket of their design. Materials will be provided to protect the egg through various means. Contestants will be judged on their design, construction, and inventiveness and win prizes during the awards ceremony after the eggs have been launched.

Engineers Ball

Engineers Ball is a semi-formal social event for UCLA Engineering Students to have fun and step away from the stress of classes. With provided food and entertainment, students will be able to enjoy catching up with old friends and making new ones.

  • Date: April 14, 2023
  • Time: 6 PM to 9 PM
  • Location: TBD

Engineers Ball is an event hosted by ESUC during our annual Engineers Week (E-Week) during Week 2 of Spring Quarter. It is a chance for engineering students to unwind from the stresses of the year and have fun with friends! With the many tasks that engineering students often take on in addition to their classes, ESUC aims to create a relaxing environment where students can dress up and have fun with crafts and games provided at the event.

Field Day

This event is an opportunity for engineering students to come together to enjoy food, games, and company.

  • Date: April 16, 2023
  • Time: 5 PM to 7 PM
  • Location: Sunset Rec Amphitheatre

The goal is for students to be able to take a break from studying, reconnect with nature, and become familiarized with their community of engineers. The community outcome will be better relationships within the engineering community that can help in improving student experiences in the classroom, in clubs, and in exploring engineering endeavors.

Art Display

Who said engineers weren't creative? Show off your art skills and have your work exhibited in the Kerckhoff Art Gallery.

  • Date: April 10-14, 2023 (All Week)
  • Time: 7 AM to 7 PM
  • Location: Kerckhoff Art Gallery + Others

Art Display is a chance for engineers to show off their artistic abilities and talents. Art created by engineers will be displayed either in person throughout the entirety of E-Week in either the ESUC Lounge, SEAS Cafe, or Kerckhoff Art Gallery AND/OR on a website and our social media. A public voting form will be posted for the people to vote for their favorite pieces, and the top 3 works will receive a prize.
