Our Story

Bruin Engineers Week (E-Week) is an annual week-long series of events hosted by ESUC during Week 2 of Spring Quarter in celebration of all things engineering.

Our Plan

Numerous events involving volunteering, networking, problem-solving, dancing, expressing ideas, and having fun will be available for students to attend.

Our Vision

Events held during Bruin E-Week are designed to bring students together through innovation, outreach, and fun as well as promote individual development.

Face the Faculty

Face off in a game of brawn and brains against your favorite UCLA Engineering faculty and staff in a team dodgeball and trivia game.

  • Date: Thursday, April 17, 2024
  • Time: Dodgeball from 11 am - 1 pm, Trivia from 4 pm - 5pm
  • Location: Dodgeball @ IM Fields, Trivia @ ESUC Lounge

Our goal is to allow students to connect with Engineering Faculty, through a friendly competition. Dodgeball will get both students and staff up off their feet and ready to work with their colleagues. Participants will also learn more about the history of UCLA, while corroborating with their teammates.

Kids Day

Are you interested in helping young kids develop an interest in engineering? Volunteer at our outreach event where elementary school students in the area are invited to participate in hands-on activities led by UCLA engineering orgs! Sign up here: https://tinyurl.com/kids-day-2023

  • Date: Wednesday, April 16, 2025
  • Time: 10 AM to 12 PM
  • Location: Engineering IV

Bruin Engineers Week is a decades-old tradition at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) hosted by the Engineering Society of UCLA. During this week-long festivities, the UCLA engineering community gathers to celebrate the achievements of the engineering clubs and works together to give back to the greater community. One of the primary events is Kids Day, during which hundreds of elementary school students are invited to UCLA for a day of hands-on learning outside of a classroom setting. This is a rewarding experience for older students to volunteer their time to the community and help foster a love for learning and engineering in younger students.

Industry Night

Meet with alumni and industry professionals in a casual setting to get a jump start on your career!

  • Date: Tuesday, April 15, 2025
  • Time: 6 PM - 8 PM
  • Location: Engineering 6 (Cohen or Mong)

This smaller networking event is embedded in Bruin E-week and is designed to be a casual, informative evening for students to start or continue forming professional relationships with company representatives/alumni, as well as get advice. The goal of Networking Night is to help undergraduate students make connections with people in the industry. Undergraduate students are able to get insight into what it is like to work in the industry and how they can get internships.

Built-A-Thon

Show off your creativity and engineering ability in a fun challenge! Work with fellow engineers to build solutions in real time, and possibly earn prizes.

  • Date: Monday, April 4th, 2025
  • Time: 5 PM to 8 PM
  • Location: Boelter 5800

In the spirit of Bruin E-Week, students will get the chance to show off their engineering skills through a Build-A-Thon! Students will form teams of engineers and practice building solutions to a given problem, testing their solution against competitors. Together, teams will receive prizes of up to $50!

Engineers' Talent Show

Engineers' Talent Show is a performance event meant to highlight the diverse skills and talents of the Samueli student body. Join as a performer for a chance to win amazing prizes and show off your skills, or attend as an audience member. Sign up here: https://tinyurl.com/e-ball-2023

  • Date: Friday, April 18, 2025
  • Time: 6 PM to 8 PM
  • Location: Kerchoff Grand Salon

Engineers Ball is an event hosted by ESUC during our annual Bruin Engineers Week (E-Week) during Week 2 of Spring Quarter. It is a chance for engineering students to unwind from the stresses of the year and have fun with friends! With the many tasks that engineering students often take on in addition to their classes, ESUC aims to create a relaxing environment where students can dress up and have fun with crafts and games provided at the event.

Art Display

Who said engineers weren't creative? Show off your art skills and have your work exhibited on Instagram and the Bruin E-Week website! Vote here: https://tinyurl.com/art-display-2023-voting

  • Date: April 14-18, 2025 (All Week)
  • Time: Whenever Your Heart Desires!
  • Location: Instagram and Bruin E-Week Website

Art Display is a chance for engineers to show off their artistic abilities and talents. Art created by engineers will be displayed either our website and social media. A public voting form will be posted for the people to vote for their favorite pieces, and the top 3 works will receive a prize.
